Our Process

How we reborn it

  1. 01

    Sourced

    We hunt estate sales, export lots, and donation drives across the city for pieces with real character.

  2. 02

    Inspected

    Every item is checked seam to seam. Stains, tears, or missing buttons mean it doesn't make the cut.

  3. 03

    Refreshed

    A wash, a steam, sometimes a stitch. Nothing structural — just enough to make it wearable again.

  4. 04

    Reborn

    Tagged, photographed, and listed once. One piece, one buyer — we never restock the same item twice.
